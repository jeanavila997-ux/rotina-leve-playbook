# Prompts para Geração de Imagens (IA)

## 🖼️ Capa do Carrossel (Slide 1)
**Objetivo:** Paz, foco e clareza mental.
- **Prompt:** `High-end professional photography, minimalist aesthetic, a peaceful person in a bright clean room with soft natural lighting, indigo and white color palette, soft focus background, hyper-realistic, 8k, wide shot, depth of field, serene atmosphere, high contrast between light and soft shadows --ar 4:5`

## 🖼️ Imagens de Apoio (Slides 2-7)
**Objetivo:** Representar a "sobrecarga" vs "clareza".
- **Sobrecarga:** `Abstract representation of mental noise, chaotic floating geometric shapes, muted grey and indigo colors, minimalist white background, cinematic lighting, high quality 3d render --ar 4:5`
- **Clareza:** `A single glowing lightbulb or a clean organized desk with a notebook, minimalist soft blue background, floating particles, ethereal lighting, professional studio shot --ar 4:5`
